SmartByte — Privacy Policy

Learn Computer Basics · Offline

Last updated: March 2026
🗄️

Data Collection

SmartByte stores your reading progress, quiz scores, and profile information locally on your device. The only data sent to the cloud is your leaderboard name, country flag, and XP score — used solely to display the global leaderboard. No other personal data is collected or shared.

🖼️

Profile Image

Your profile picture is stored locally on your device only and is never uploaded to any server.

🔥

Firebase (Google)

SmartByte uses Firebase, a service provided by Google LLC, for the global leaderboard feature. Firebase may collect anonymous usage data and device identifiers. Learn more:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Privacy Information
📢

Advertising — Google AdMob

SmartByte displays ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may use your device's advertising ID to show relevant ads. You can opt out of personalised ads in your device Settings → Google → Ads.

  • AdMob Privacy Policy
  • Google Advertising Policies
🔑

Permissions

  • Camera Roll — only when you choose to set a profile picture
  • Notifications — only for optional daily learning reminders
  • No other permissions are requested
👶

Children's Privacy

SmartByte is intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided data, please contact us.

📋

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated with app updates. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of any changes. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
